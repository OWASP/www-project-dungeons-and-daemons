# The Dark Tangent

**Real name:** Jeff
**Role:** Conference Founder & All-Knowing Oracle
**Background:** Jeff built DEF CON from a farewell party into the world's largest hacker gathering. He alone saw the Short-Sellers during the setup phase and knows exactly who is sabotaging his life's work. Unfortunately, every one of those saboteurs has private GMs from him that would destroy his reputation if leaked. He must guide his team in secret.

**Special Ability — Off the Record:**
Once per game, Jeff may alert a Loyalist player. The alert may name one suspicious player or describe one active threat without being attributed to him. If Jeff ever publicly accuses a Short-Seller by name, the Short-Sellers immediately play the "Dox" card and Jeff is eliminated from the game.
