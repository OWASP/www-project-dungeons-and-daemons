# The NOC Engineer — "Packet Sniffer"

**Real name:** Jamie
**Role:** Network Operations Center Senior Volunteer
**Background:** Jamie keeps the network alive for 30,000 hackers who are simultaneously trying to attack it. They can read a Wireshark capture the way others read a newspaper. If something bad is happening on the wire, Jamie will find it — eventually.

**Special Ability — Deep Packet Inspection:**
Once per game, Jamie can demand to see the access logs. The GM reveals the category of the threat that may cause a failure (e.g., Digital & Wireless, Baiting, Obstruction) but not the specific card or suspect.

**Stat Modifiers:** +1 to any Network pillar roll. +1 to Digital & Wireless threat mitigation.
