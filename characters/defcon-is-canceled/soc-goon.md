# The SOC Goon — "Radio Check"

**Real name:** Alex
**Role:** Security Operations Center Volunteer
**Background:** Alex has been a Goon for six years and has seen everything. Crowd crushes, fed sightings, rogue Wi-Fi pineapples in the speaker lounge. Alex trusts nobody completely and keeps a personal radio log of everyone's movements during setup day.

**Special Ability — Radio Check:**
Once per game, Alex can call a "Radio Check" on two players. The GM privately tells Alex whether the two players' deployment votes have consistently helped the pillars (i.e., the GM confirms whether one of them is a Short-Seller, but not which one). This ability cannot be used during a pillar vote itself — only between events.

**Stat Modifiers:** +1 to any Physical threat mitigation.
