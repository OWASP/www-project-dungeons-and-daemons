# The Village Lead — "Community Alert"

**Real name:** Jordan
**Role:** Hacker Village Coalition Coordinator
**Background:** Jordan runs coordination between the dozens of independent hacker villages — Aerospace, Biohacking, Car Hacking, ICS, and more. They have the trust of every village organizer on the floor and can mobilize a community of experts in minutes when something goes wrong.

**Special Ability — Community Alert:**
Once per game, Jordan can call a "Community Alert" before a deployment team is locked in for a mission. The group votes again on one player's inclusion in the team. This forces a re-nomination without the accused player being able to self-nominate for that same mission.

**Stat Modifiers:** +1 to The Party pillar and +1 to Manipulation & Elicitation threat mitigation.
