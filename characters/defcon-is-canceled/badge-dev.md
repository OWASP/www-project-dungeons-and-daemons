# The Badge Dev — "Firmware Patch"

**Real name:** Morgan
**Role:** Electronic Badge Lead Developer
**Background:** Morgan has been designing the DEF CON badge since 2021. It's a cryptographic puzzle box, a community, and a piece of art all at once. This year's badge is the most complex ever built, which also makes it the most vulnerable. Morgan will not let anyone brick it.

**Special Ability — Emergency Patch:**
Once per game, Morgan can apply an "Emergency Firmware Patch" to The Badge pillar. This negates one failed roll on a Badge mission (the roll counts as a 7 instead), preventing a strike. This ability cannot save a pillar that already has two strikes.

**Stat Modifiers:** +1 to The Badge pillar. +1 to Baiting threat mitigation (hardware-focused).
