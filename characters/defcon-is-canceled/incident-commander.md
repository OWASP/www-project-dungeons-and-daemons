# The Incident Commander — "War Room"

**Real name:** Sam
**Role:** Cross-Pillar Crisis Coordinator
**Background:** Sam spent years bridging the gap between Goons, NOC, and village leads whenever incidents hit multiple teams at once. They are not the loudest person in the room, but when radio traffic turns chaotic, Sam can cut through the noise, assign owners, and keep the right people on the right problem. In a conference where one bad decision can cascade across every pillar, that discipline saves lives.

**Special Ability — War Room:**
Once per game, immediately after a mission failure, Sam may call a "War Room" and stabilize operations before the next event begins. The team ignores the normal -1 penalty from that failure, and no negative carry-over modifier is applied to the next mission.

**Stat Modifiers:** +1 to Location Spoofing threat mitigation.
